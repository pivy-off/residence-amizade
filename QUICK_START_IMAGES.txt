QUICK START: Adding Your Images & Videos

1. HERO SECTION:
   - Add hero-image.jpg to: public/images/
   - (Optional) Add hero-video.mp4 to: public/images/

2. ROOM IMAGES:
   - Add photos to: public/images/room-[room-type]/
   - Update content/data.json with all image paths

3. GALLERY:
   - Add photos to: public/images/gallery/

After adding files, the website will automatically use them!

See IMAGE_SETUP.md for detailed instructions.
